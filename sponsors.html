Why Support Us?

Solar Browser is developed by a dedicated team of high school students from Türkiye. We're not using any code from existing browsers - we're writing all engines (web rendering engine, JavaScript engine, etc.) by ourselves from scratch. Your support allows us to:

  • Accelerate Development

    Fund new features and improvements to make Solar even better.

  • Remain Independent

    Help us stay free from corporate influence and focused on user privacy.

  • Advance Research

    Support our ongoing research into browser security and performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is my sponsorship used?

Your sponsorship directly funds development, server costs, security audits, and allows our team to dedicate more time to improving Solar Browser.

Can I sponsor as an individual and remain anonymous?

Absolutely! During the sponsorship process, you can choose to remain anonymous or use a pseudonym in our public supporters list.

Is my sponsorship tax-deductible?

This depends on your location and tax laws. We recommend consulting with a tax professional for advice specific to your situation.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my sponsorship tier?

Yes, you can change your sponsorship tier at any time through your account dashboard.

How do I access my sponsor benefits?

After becoming a sponsor, you'll receive an email with instructions on how to access your benefits through our supporter portal.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions?

Yes! We offer special sponsorship rates for non-profits, educational institutions, and open source projects. Please contact us for details.
