The Future of Web Browsing

Solar was created with a vision: to develop a browser entirely from scratch, without using any existing engines like Chromium or Gecko. Our team of high school students from Türkiye is building everything from the JavaScript engine to the rendering system.

With Solar, you're not just browsing the web – you're experiencing a browser where every line of code was written by students with passion and innovation.
